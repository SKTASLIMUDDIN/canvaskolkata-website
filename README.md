## 📋 About

Canvas Kolkata is a Kolkata-based marketing and printing services agency offering affordable, results-driven marketing solutions for businesses, political parties, NGOs, and individuals across India.

### Services
- 📱 Social Media Management
- 📈 Digital Marketing & SEO
- 📣 Campaign Management
- 🎯 META & Google Ads
- 👕 Print Design & Merchandise (T-Shirts, Caps, Badges)
- 💡 Brand Strategy & Consulting

## 🏗️ Tech Stack

- **HTML5** — Semantic markup with full SEO optimization
- **CSS3** — Custom design system with glassmorphism, gradients, and animations
- **Vanilla JavaScript** — No frameworks, lightweight and fast
- **Google Fonts** — Outfit & Inter
- **Font Awesome 6** — Icon library
