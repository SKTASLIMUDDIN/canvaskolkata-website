Our Journey

The Canvas Kolkata Story

Canvas Kolkata was born out of a simple yet powerful belief — that every brand, regardless of size or budget, deserves access to world-class marketing. Founded in Kolkata, the cultural capital of India, our agency set out to bridge the gap between premium marketing services and affordable pricing.

What started as a small team of passionate creatives working on local businesses has grown into a full-service marketing agency serving clients across India. From helping startups build their digital presence to managing large-scale political campaigns and empowering NGOs with impactful outreach — we've done it all, and we've loved every moment of it.

Today, with over 500 projects delivered and 200+ happy clients, Canvas Kolkata stands as a testament to what's possible when creativity meets affordability. We continue to push boundaries, adopt new technologies, and find innovative ways to help our clients achieve their goals. Our canvas keeps growing, and we invite you to be part of our story.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower businesses and organizations with affordable, innovative marketing solutions that drive real results. We believe that effective marketing shouldn't be a luxury — it should be accessible to every brand that has a story worth telling.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and accessible marketing partner, helping brands of all sizes paint their success stories. We envision a future where every business, political party, and NGO can harness the full power of marketing without financial barriers.
