FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What services does Canvas Kolkata offer?

Canvas Kolkata offers a comprehensive range of marketing services including social media management, digital marketing & SEO, political and NGO campaign management, META & Google Ads, print design & merchandise, brand strategy, and video content creation. We serve businesses, political parties, and NGOs across India.

How affordable are your services compared to competitors?

Our pricing is designed to be significantly more affordable than traditional marketing agencies — often up to 60% less — without compromising on quality. We believe premium marketing should be accessible to every business, regardless of size. Our Starter plan begins at just ₹4,999/month.

Do you work with political parties and NGOs?

Absolutely! We have extensive experience working with political parties and NGOs. We offer specialized campaign management services including voter outreach, event promotion, social media campaigns, merchandise and print design for rallies, and targeted digital advertising to help amplify your message.

What is the turnaround time for print orders?

Standard print orders are typically completed within 5–7 business days, depending on the complexity and quantity. Rush orders can be fulfilled in 2–3 business days for an additional fee. We handle everything from business cards and brochures to large banners, t-shirts, and campaign merchandise.

Can I get a custom marketing plan?

Yes! We understand that every business is unique. If our standard plans don't perfectly fit your needs, we'll create a tailored marketing package just for you. Simply contact us via WhatsApp or fill out our contact form, and our team will craft a custom proposal within 24 hours.

Do you offer bulk printing discounts?

Yes, we offer significant discounts on bulk printing orders. Whether you need thousands of flyers for a campaign, branded merchandise for events, or large quantities of business materials, we provide competitive bulk pricing. Contact us for a custom bulk order quote.
